Some of my Favorites

Two of my favorite movies consist of The Matrix and Alien. The Matrix by the Wachowskis, was a fantastic movie. I just recently watched it for the first time over the summer, and it was an experience. I really liked the plot of it, and how each cast member performed in it, especially Keanu Reeves. I thought the story was very fascinating, with it throwing all sorts of twists right at me. It was a very fun movie to watch

I also enjoyed the movie Alien, by Ridley Scott. I had also never seen this movie until the summer of 2017, when I decided to go on a movie binge. I watched this and I was instantly hooked. I like movies like this with a closed-in atmosphere, where the main characters cannot escape. This type of atmosphere makes the movie more suspenseful. Like the last movie, I really enjoyed all the performances, especially Sigourney Weaver as Ripley. This movie was a blast and holds its ranks as one of my favorite movies of all time.
